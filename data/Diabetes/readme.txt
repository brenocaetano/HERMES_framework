Diabetes Dataset

The empirical validation of the HERMES framework utilizes the Diabetes 130-US Hospitals dataset publicly available at the UCI Machine Learning Repository:

Diabetes 130-US Hospitals for Years 1999-2008 Dataset](https://archive.ics.uci.edu/dataset/296/diabetes+130-us+hospitals+for+years+1999-2008)

Description: A clinical dataset containing hospital encounters, originally aimed at predicting patient readmission. In this work, it was utilized to evaluate the framework's capability to discover meaningful latent classes in a multi-objective environment (minimizing/maximizing specific clinical indicators).
